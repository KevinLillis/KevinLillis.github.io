Details for Spring 2020 Final Exams

  • Final exams for all of my classes will be through Blackboard.
  • The final exams will be given at the scheduled exam time provided by the Registrar's Office.
  • Each exam will become available on Blackboard at its scheduled time.
  • The final exam will be a timed exam. You will have the entire scheduled time to complete the exam. If you haven't already submitted your exam by the end of the alotted time, it will be submitted automatically.
  • All questions are to be answered directly through Blackboard. There will be no need to upload files containing your written answers.
  • I will maintain an open WebEx session during the exam. You can send a PRIVATE message to me if you have any questions during the exam.

  • Details about the content of the final exams are provided on each classes' Class Website.
