Homework 02 — Car Cost

Write a program that determines the "Total Cost of Ownership" for a car over a 5-year period. Your program should prompt the user for the following:

  • Purchase price of the car
  • Miles that will be driven in a year
  • Gas price (Simplifying Assumption: gas prices don't change over the 5-year period)
  • Fuel efficiency of the car (miles per gallon)
  • Resale value of the car after 5 years

After reading in the above inputs, your program should compute the total cost of owning the car for the 5-year period.

Do some research on at least two hybrid cars. Then run your program one time for each car, using today's gas price and 15,000 miles per year.
