Homework 04 — Two Circles

Write a Java program that prompts the user to enter the center coordinates and radius of two circles. The program should then display which of the following situations exists:

  • The smaller circle is completely inside of the larger circle.
  • The smaller circle is completely outside of the larger circle.
  • The two circles overlap (two circles that touch at exactly one point are considered overlapping).
