HW 06 - Two Circles

Write a program that prompts the user to enter the center coordinates and radius of two circles. The program should then display which of the following situations exists (see figure):

  • The smaller circle is completely inside of the larger circle.
  • The smaller circle is completely outside of the larger circle.
  • The two circles overlap (two circles that touch at exactly one point are considered overlapping).

Below are three samples of the program. It may help if you use graph paper and draw the circles from the three examples below.


     Circle 1 - Center is (x1, y1), radius is r1
     Enter x1: 4
     Enter y1: 4
     Enter r1: 4
     Circle 2 - Center is (x2, y2), radius is r2
     Enter x2: 4
     Enter y2: 2
     Enter r2: 1
