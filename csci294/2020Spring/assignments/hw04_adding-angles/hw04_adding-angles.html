Homework 04 – Adding Angles

Angles are often measured in degrees (°), minutes ('), and seconds ("). There are 360 degrees in a circle, 60 minutes in one degree, and 60 seconds in one minute. Write a program that reads from the keyboard two angular measurements given in degrees, minutes, and seconds and then calculates and prints their sum. Use your program to verify the following: 

  74° 29' 13" + 105° 8' 16" = 179° 37' 29"

  7° 14' 55" + 5° 24' 55" = 12° 39' 50"

  20° 31' 19" + 0° 31' 30" = 21° 2' 49"

  122° 17' 48" + 237° 42' 12" = 0° 0' 0"

Sample Runs
