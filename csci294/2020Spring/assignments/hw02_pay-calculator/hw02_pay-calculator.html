Homework 02 – Pay Calculator

For this assignment you are to write a C++ program that calculates the gross pay for an hourly worker, who is paid an hourly rate plus time-and-a-half for overtime hours (more than 40 hours in a week). Your program should prompt the user for the total number of hours worked and the hourly rate. It should then calculate and display the gross pay.

Sample Runs

Below are two examples of how your program should look when it runs. The things that the user types in are shown in green. You program does not need to use any colors. Other than that though, your program should look like the sample runs. 


                 Enter number of hours worked: 37
                 Enter hourly rate: 10
                 Gross pay is 370
         
             


                 Enter number of hours worked: 41
                 Enter hourly rate: 10
                 Gross pay is 415
