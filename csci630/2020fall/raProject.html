Risk Assessment Project

Overview

This project involves defining a fictional enterprise with various business functions. Students will identify information technology assets and functions to support the business needs of the enterprise. A qualitative and quantitative risk assessment for a subset of the IT assets will be conducted. Lastly a information security policy for employees of the enterprise will be developed.
