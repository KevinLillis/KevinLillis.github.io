HW 02 – Compliance Requirement Exercise

Due Date: Sun 9/20

Investigate three different security/privacy regulatory requirements including: GDPR, HIPAA, and one other of your choice. Explain the basic requirements and the encumbered entities for each. Then list and describe commonalities and differences among them.

Readings

  • GDPR
  • HIPAA
  • Others as needed

This is an individual exercise. You will several weeks to complete this, but you will have other assignments in parallel so budget your time carefully.

Deliverables

  • 1 page summary of each compliance requirement (3 pages total).
  • 3 page description of the differences and similarities among the three requirements.
  • A separate document showing all references and sources you reviewed for this assignment.

Note

Do not copy & paste information from documents used for your research. You must summarize details in your own words.
