HW 03 – Quantitative and Qualitative Risk Analysis

Due Date: Sun 9/27

This is an individual assignment.

You are to use two of the assets from your RA Project enterprise. For one you are to perform a quantitative Risk Analysis and for the other perform a qualitative Risk Analysis. You should select one specific threat for each asset that you are working with.

Deliverables

A Risk Analysis report for each asset that includes:

  1. Risk Analysis conclusion and results
  2. Methodology and all formulas, charts, matrices, assumptions used
  3. Annotated listing of all sources of information used to find or calculate the final Risk Analysis results
