Group Policy Assignment

Final Assignment

  1. Your group is to use the individual Personnel Policies already submitted and create a single policy that uses the best of the individual policies.
  2. You are to create a Remote Access Policy to cover how users or employees (as appropriate) access your systems.
  3. Create a Procedure that covers how to remotely access your systems.

Both policies must be structured and formatted the same. There are numerous policy templates available and you are encouraged to study them. However, your group must write your policies and tailor them to your corporation. The source of any material you use must be credited as you would in a research paper.

The procedure should follow the same formatting style as your policies.

Pay close attention to the differences between policies and procedures as described in your text and elsewhere.

This is a group assignment. You will have some time in class to discuss the policy requirements.
