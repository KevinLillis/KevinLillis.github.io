Risk Assessment Assignment 1 — Enterprise Description

Define a fictional enterprise that will be used for your group Risk Assessment exercise and a few individual assignments.

The enterprise should be of medium size with multiple locations and enough business functions to provide for a good variety of potential threats and asset types. You must include, at least, a cloud component, multiple locations, in-house networking, staff working at multiple levels (management, clerical, etc.).

For tonight you are to define as much of your enterprise as possible.

  • Describe the basic purpose of your enterprise
  • Describe the physical structure of your enterprise
    • Include locations, number of employees, etc.
  • List the major business functions of your enterprise
