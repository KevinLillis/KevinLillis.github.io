Risk Assessment — Asset Assignment

General Description

This is a group project.

You are to use two of the assets from your enterprise. For one you are to perform a quantitative Risk Analysis and for the other perform a qualitative Risk Analysis. You should select one specific threat for each asset that you are working with.

Deliverables

A Risk Analysis report for each asset that includes:

  1. Risk Analysis conclusion and results
  2. Methodology and all formulas, charts, matrices, assumptions used
  3. Annotated listing of all sources of information used to find or calculate the final Risk Analysis results
  4. Description of the contribution of each group member

Due Date and Submission
