Compliance Requirement Exercise

Summary

Investigate three different security/privacy compliance requirements.

Assignment Details

Investigate three different security/privacy regulatory requirements including: GDPR, HIPAA, and one other of your choice. Explain the basic requirements and the encumbered entities for each. Then list and describe commonalities and differences among them.

Readings

  • GDPR
  • HIPAA
  • Others as needed

Work methodology

This is an individual exercise. You will have 4-5 weeks to complete this, but you will have other assignments in parallel so budget your time carefully.

Due Date

Tentative due date is October 3.

Deliverables

  • 2-3 page summary of each compliance requirement.
  • 4-5 page description of the differences and similarities among the three requirements.
  • A separate docuent showing all references and sources you reviewed for this assignment.

Note

Do not copy & paste information from documents used for your research. You must summarize details in your own words.
