Notes About Buffon's Needle

An Algorithm to estimate the value of 𝝅

  1. Drop a 1-inch need on a sheet of paper with lines 2 inches apart
  2. If the needs touches a line, that counts as a hit
  3. The value (drops / hits) is an estimate of 𝝅

Needle Vertical Position

  • Generate random d between 0 and 2.
  • d is the distance from the lower end of the needle to the next line up.

Needle Angle

  • Generate random angle θ between 0 and 180°
  • θ is the angle of the needle.

Detecting a Hit

  • The needle hits the line when d ≤ sin θ
