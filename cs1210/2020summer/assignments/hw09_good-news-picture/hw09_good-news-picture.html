HW 09 – Good News Picture

With the stress of COVID-19 and the lockdown, along with our current civil unrest, I thought it would be nice for everyone to focus on something positive. So for this assignment I would like you to use Python's turtle module to create a picture of something positive and uplifting that has either happened to you, someone you know, or even just something you've heard about.

Be creative. See if you can make your picture look good and be inspirational. The size of the picture should be at least 500 × 500 and the window should have an appropriate title. You may want to look at the online documentation for the Python turtle module.

Tell the Story

In your program file, please include a paragraph that describes your picture and tells the story. This should be done using a triple-quoted string, similar to the way we've been doing docstrings.

Program Organization

Your program should be organized and include a main() function as well as a makeTurtle() function like we've done in class.
