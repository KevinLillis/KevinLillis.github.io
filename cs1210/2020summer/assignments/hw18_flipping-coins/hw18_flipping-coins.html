HW 18 – Flipping Coins

For this assignment you will simulate flipping a coin and then look at the results.

Assignment

When your program is executed, it should display the following menu:

        Let's Flip Some Coins
        
           1) Flip a coin one time
           2) Flip a coin n times and show the flips
           3) Flip a coin n times and show the ratio of heads to tails
           4) Flip a coin n times and show the longest run of heads and tails
           5) Likelihood of a run of 4 or more heads with 20 flips
        
           Selection:


Your program should use two global variables called H and T defined as follows 

H = 0
T = 1

0) Invalid menu selection

Your program should terminate if an invalid menu selection is entered.

5) Likelihood of a run of 4 or more heads with 20 flips

Here, your program is to determine the likelihood of getting a run of 4 or more heads when 20 coins are flipped. This is done by running the experiment of flipping 20 coins many times. The result should be the percent of experiments where there was a run of four or more heads. The result is displayed with two digits after the decimal points.
