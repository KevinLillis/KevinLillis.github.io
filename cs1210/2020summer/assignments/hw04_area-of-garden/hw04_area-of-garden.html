HW 04 – Area of Garden

A landscape and design company has been asked to design a rectangular garden that has a sidewalk around the perimeter and a crosswalk through the middle of the garden. The length of the garden is ℓ, the width of the garden is w, and the width of the sidewalk is d, as shown in the below picture.

Write a program that prompts the user for the dimensions of the of the garden and the width of the sidewalk and then outputs the area of the grass.

Your program should be organized and utilize a main() function like the programs we have written in class.

Sample Run

Note that the user input is shown in green only to make it easier to see how the program behaves. Your program does not need to produce any colors.


     Enter the length of the garden in feet: 28 
     Enter the width of the garden in feet: 11
     Enter the width of the sidewalk in feet: 2

     The grassy area of the garden is 110 square feet.
