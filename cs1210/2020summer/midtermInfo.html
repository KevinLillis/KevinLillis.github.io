Midterm Exam Information

Date and Time

The midterm exam will be on Thursday, July 9. You will have from 11:30 AM – 12:00 Noon to start the exam.

Exam Format

The midterm exam will be an online exam administered through Blackboard. It will be a timed exam and the timer will start when you begin the exam. The exam questions will be similar in nature to the quiz questions you have already seen. This is an open book open notes exam.

What the Exam Will Cover

The midterm exam will cover the following:

  • All of Chapter 1 from the textbook
  • All of Chapter 2 from the textbook
  • All material covered during class lectures and discussion sections
  • All sample programs written during lectures
  • All material addressed in Homework and Lab assignments
  • All items from the quizzes
