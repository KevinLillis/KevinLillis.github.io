HW 04 - Father's Day Picture

Sunday is Father's Day. For this assignment you are to use Python's turtle module to create a picture in honor of this holiday. Please research Father's Day if you are unfamiliar with the holiday. You should also research on-line documentation and examples that discuss the various features of Python's turtle module.

You may be as creative as you wish, but try to make a visually appealing picture. Here are the specific requirements:

  • The size of the picture should be 500 × 500.
  • The window should have an appropriate title.
  • The drawing itself should include a text message.
  • Your picture must use at least four different colors in addition to the background color.
  • Your picture should have at least two distinct objects (a person, a tree, a flower, etc.)

What to Turn In

A Dropbox Folder has been created for this assignment in ICON. Please submit your Python program through ICON using that folder.
