HW 04 — Father's Day Picture

Last Sunday was Father's Day. For this assignment you are to use Python's turtle module to create a picture in honor of this holiday. Please research Father's Day if you are unfamiliar with the holiday. You should also research on-line documentation and examples that discuss the various features of Python's turtle module.

You may be as creative as you wish, but try to make a visually appealing picture. Here are the specific requirements:

  • The size of the picture should be 500 × 500.
  • The window should have an appropriate title.
  • The drawing itself should include a text message.
  • Your picture must use at least four different colors in addition to the background color.
  • Your picture should have at least two distinct objects (a person, a tree, a flower, etc.)
  • Be creative and try to make your picture look good

Grading

Please review the Grading Rubric for Programming Assignments. Also, please remember to include the header information at the beginning of your program.
