Notes About Buffon's Needle

  • An algorithm to estimate the value of pi

  • Drop a 1-inch need on a sheet of paper
    • with lines 2 inches apart
  • If the needs touches a line, that counts as a hit

  • The value (drops / hits) is an estimate of pi

Needle Position

  • Needle length = 1, distance between lines = 2

  • Generate random d  between 0 and 2
    • d is the distance from the lower end of the needle to the next line
  • Generate random angle θ between 0 and 180 degrees
    • θ is the angle between the needle and the line
  • The needle hits the line when d ≤ sin θ
